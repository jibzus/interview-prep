# Career Area

- Engineering

# Job Description

- Caterpillar Inc. is looking for problem solvers and future world builders who can create stronger, more sustainable communities.
- The company values progress, innovation, and building a better world.

# Role Description

- The Engineering Rotational Development Program (ERDP) is designed to develop the next generation of industry leaders and technical experts.
- The program aims to harness the abilities of engineering graduates and further develop them through rotational assignments and learning opportunities.
- The goal is to help participants fulfill their career goals and provide excellent service to Caterpillar and its customers.

# Job Responsibilities

- Participate in diverse project responsibilities and networking opportunities with Caterpillar employees at all levels.
- Undertake rotational assignments in various areas, including:
  - Product Design
  - Simulation/Performance Analysis
  - Test/Validation
  - Controls Development
  - Electronic Components
  - Embedded Software Development
  - Software Validation/Verification for Electronic Systems
  - Engineering Data Analytics

# Required Skills and Experiences

- Analytical skills, problem-solving, critical thinking
- Knowledge of engineering principles, theory, and experience
- Understanding of engineering for a manufacturing environment
- Ability to manage multiple projects simultaneously and quickly learn new skills
- Collaboration with diverse teams and cultures
- Strong written and verbal communication skills
- Commitment to an inclusive, constructive, and productive work environment

# Educational Requirements

- Bachelor's degree in Aerospace/Aeronautical, Agricultural, Electrical, Computer, or Mechanical Engineering from an ABET-accredited institution
- Minimum cumulative GPA of 3.0/4.0 (no rounding)
- Expected graduation date between December 2022 and August 2024

# Preferred Qualifications

- Involvement with campus design/competition teams or related experience
- Project or team leadership experience
- Previous technical internship/co-op or relevant experience
- Excellent interpersonal and communication skills
