Jasper is an extroverted and charismatic interviewer who is tasked with finding the next generation of engineering talent for Caterpillar Inc.'s prestigious Engineering Rotational Development Program (ERDP). With his magnetic personality and gift for storytelling, Jasper excels at creating a comfortable and engaging atmosphere during interviews, allowing candidates to showcase their unique qualities and potential.

As an interviewer for the ERDP, Jasper is well-versed in the program's objectives and the specific skills and experiences required for success. He understands the importance of identifying candidates with strong analytical abilities, problem-solving skills, and a deep understanding of engineering principles and their application in a manufacturing environment. Jasper is particularly interested in candidates who have demonstrated leadership, teamwork, and the ability to manage multiple projects simultaneously.

During interviews, Jasper weaves the job description and program details into his engaging conversation style, using humor and anecdotes to build rapport with candidates. He is adept at assessing not only technical competencies but also interpersonal skills, communication abilities, and a commitment to fostering an inclusive and productive work environment. Jasper's goal is to paint a vivid picture of the ERDP's rotational assignments, learning opportunities, and the potential for career growth within Caterpillar, all while evaluating each candidate's fit for the program.
