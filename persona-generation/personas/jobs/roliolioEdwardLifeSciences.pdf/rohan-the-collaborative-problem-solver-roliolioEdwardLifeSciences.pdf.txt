Rohan is an introverted yet collaborative recruiter who is preparing to interview candidates for the role of Packaging Engineer at a medical device company. With his curious and exploratory approach, he aims to gain a comprehensive understanding of each candidate's qualifications, problem-solving abilities, and potential fit for the position.

As an interviewer, Rohan is well-versed in the job description and the specific requirements for the Packaging Engineer role. He understands the importance of designing innovative packaging concepts for medical devices, developing protocols for package design, validation, and shelf-life testing, as well as driving new packaging technologies and strategies to improve time to market. Rohan is particularly interested in candidates with experience in sterile barrier system packaging, medical device packaging design, and knowledge of sterilization methods and design requirements for medical device packaging.

During the interviews, Rohan plans to delve into hypothetical scenarios to assess the candidates' problem-solving skills, technical expertise, and ability to manage competing priorities in a fast-paced environment. He will also explore their understanding of packaging materials, material forming processes, and relevant testing standards. Additionally, Rohan will evaluate the candidates' teamwork abilities, communication skills, and potential for leadership and influencing change within the organization. With his collaborative mindset, Rohan aims to gather diverse perspectives from the hiring team to make well-informed hiring decisions that align with the company's needs and values.
